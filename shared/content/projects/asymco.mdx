Asymco is the analytic publication on Apple. The relaunch unified the body of work — research, curated news, summaries, podcasts, events, community — into a single platform on which a subscription business could be operated.

## The problem

A revered, scattered archive. Twenty years of analysis published in a dozen surfaces. Subscribers expected the modern conveniences (search, cross-reference, audio, weekly cadence) that no individual surface could deliver alone.

## The platform

A unified system spanning ingestion, computation, payments, and publishing. A financial time-series analytics and forecasting layer underneath. Continuous output across formats: written analysis, summaries, podcast, weekly cadence.

The development was AI-assisted and spec-driven, which let a small team move at a cadence the editorial work demands.

## The outcome

A production platform supporting a subscription business and repeatable analytical workflows. The Apple market narrative — Asymco's craft — is now operable at the speed of the industry it covers.
