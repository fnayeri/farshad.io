A compliance-tracking system for one of the most quietly serious hedge funds in the world. Baupost's research often preceded execution by months. The constraints on a deal — regulatory, structural, counterparty — could change between the research and the trade. The system kept those constraints visible, current, and actionable.

## The shape of the problem

Constraints on hedges had to evolve with the regulatory environment and the complexity of the instrument. They also had to be usable by three different audiences: researchers, compliance staff, management. The product surface was the place those three audiences met.

## What shipped

A compliance system that prevented regulatory breaches and made the firm's investment process auditable end to end. Operational transparency without sacrificing the speed researchers needed.

## What it confirmed

Serious finance work rewards the combination of careful systems thinking and a builder who has spent time inside the artifact. Generic enterprise software does not survive contact with this kind of operator.
