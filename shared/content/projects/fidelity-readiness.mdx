Fidelity's institutional sales team needed a way to turn retirement-readiness data into a conversation with Plan Sponsors at Fortune-100 employers. The deck was the wrong artifact. The right artifact was a directed scene a salesperson could run live in front of a CHRO.

## What we built

A native iPad application driven by Fidelity's customer data, built on the Pixxa Perspective platform. Salespeople could pivot live: from a workforce snapshot to a multi-decade trend, from one demographic slice to another, with the cinematography directing attention to what mattered.

## Why it worked

Fidelity stopped being a service provider in those conversations. They became the operator who could see the customer's workforce more clearly than the customer could. The conversation moved from discounts to planning.

## The lineage

This is the pattern: when the data is yours and the customer cares, the right tool is a direction layer on top of a serious platform — not a deck.
