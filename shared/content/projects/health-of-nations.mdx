A global public-health platform spanning a hundred years of demographic data — 1950 to 2050 — across all countries. The platform is the substrate; the stage piece is the public face.

## The data

Heterogeneous, fragmented, and dirty. AI methods drive cleaning, anomaly detection, and reconciliation across schemas that were never designed to be compared.

## The stage piece

A three-act presentation: *Lifespans*, *Lives of Nations*, and *Health of a Nation*. The closing act asks a question: if we do not address the diseases of prosperity, what does the next century look like?

Presented at the Global Health Exhibition 2025 (Riyadh) and to ~200 senior leaders at the Saudi Ministry of Health in early 2026.

## What it establishes

Data cinematography at planetary scale. The platform makes a hundred years of demographic data sit still long enough to be looked at as a single story.
