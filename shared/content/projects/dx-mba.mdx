Worked directly with Clayton Christensen and Dave Sundahl on the productization of Disruptive Innovation as a teachable and applicable system.

## What we built

DisruptiveInnovation.com began as a community surface for innovation researchers — academic and operator — to collaborate on theory and on cases. The work later reformulated as **dx.mba**, a Disruptive MBA / Product Science Lab.

## Why it matters

Christensen's theory has had its moment as a vocabulary. The harder work is operationalizing it inside teams that want to *use* it — not cite it. The dx.mba lineage is the place that work continues.

## My role

Sat at the seam between Christensen's framing and the artifact a community could rally around. Picked the surface, picked the cadence, picked the shape of the curriculum. Built the platform that made it run.
