iGEN Corporation has been the operating vehicle since 1998. Independent. Small. Project-shape over headcount-shape. The clients have been serious — Verizon, Fidelity, Pfizer, EY, Baupost, CME — and the engagements have ranged from platform builds to advisory work.

## What it lets the work look like

A founder-operator with the freedom to take on the engagements where the substrate of the problem matters more than the size of the team. iGEN is not a body shop. It is one principal and a small senior bench, built to deliver platform-grade work end-to-end.

## What has been built under it

The Verizon Labs QoS platform. The CME Pivot trade-clearing component. The Fidelity Retirement Readiness sales platform. The early App Store apps. The work with the Christensen Institute. Pieces of the Pixxa platform.

## Why it survives

Patience. The clients who hire iGEN repeatedly are clients who believe long horizons compound. The work that pays off in year five is the work iGEN exists to do.
