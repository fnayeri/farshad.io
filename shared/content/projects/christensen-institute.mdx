Worked directly with Clayton Christensen of Harvard Business School on a community for innovation researchers — academic and practitioner — to collaborate on disruption theory and its application.

## The product

DisruptiveInnovation.com: a community platform that preserved the integrity of the foundational theory while giving working operators a place to bring cases, debate framings, and apply the vocabulary to their own industries.

## The lineage

The community work seeded the dx.MBA / Product Science Lab effort. Christensen's framings continue to shape the strategy practice; the community surface was where they were stress-tested by the people who had to *use* them.
