management research community product strategy innovation

Problem

Professor Clay Christensen of Harvard Business School envisioned a community of innovation researchers at the Institute.

Stakes

To propagate disruptive innovation concepts, an accessible community had to emerge. The Institute needed to foster collaboration on both theoretical and practical applications among academics and practitioners.

Discovery

The system had to preserve the integrity of foundational theories while ensuring accessibility to diverse audiences.

Outcome

Launched a community system, DisruptiveInnovation.org, promoting innovation research, collaboration, and the application of disruptive principles. Insights from this effort were later integrated into dx.MBA.
