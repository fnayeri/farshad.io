Clay Christensen Institute

Worked directly with Clay Christensen (Harvard Business School) to productize his vision for collaborative business innovation, later reformulated into dx.MBA innovation platform.

Problem

Professor Clay Christensen of Harvard Business School had a vision for a community of innovation researchers at the Institute.

Stakes

To propagate disruptive innovation concepts, and accessible community had to emerge. To do this, the institute needed to foster collaboration on both theory and practical applications across academics and practitioners.

Discovery

The system had to preserve the integrity of foundational theories while ensuring accessibility to diverse audiences.

Outcome

Launched a community system: DisruptiveInnovation.org, promoting innovation research, collaboration, and application of disruptive principles. Principles from this effort were later integrated into dx.MBA.
