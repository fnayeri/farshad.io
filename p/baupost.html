Product Lead
New Investment Warning System
Baupost Group

Led design & development of a system to track compliance constraints on hedge fund investments.

Problem

Hedge funds often have to wait on specific conditions before they can execute deals. This means research for a deal may proceed the execution by many months. Yet, the deal still has to go through compliance, and meet other constraints.

Stakes

Executing deals with invalid compliance constraints may result in major Financial and reputitional risk to the firm.

Discovery

To adopt to the evolving regulatory environments and complex investment instruments, constraints on a hedge may vary. This had to be balanced against simplicity of use by a broad class of users: researches, compliance staff, and management.

Outcome

Implemented a compliance tracking system, protecting the firm from regulatory breaches and enhancing operational transparency.
