What Linguistic Categories Reveal about AI

AI chatbots are masters of syntax: fluent, fast, and positively confident. But exuberant fluency is not the same as understanding. Beneath AI chatbots' eloquence lies no grasp of meaning or causality. Human conversations, by contrast, are the result of interplay between syntax, semantics, and pragmatics: words, meanings, and intentions.

Using a Cognitive Linguistics lens can help explain the profound effects of this dichotomy on how we interact with AI, and how designers are evolving AI interfaces to compensate.
