Built a suite of talent management apps for iPad for augmenting clients HRM SaaS offering.

## Problem
Peoplefluent needed to enhance its HRM SaaS offering with mobile solutions to meet modern workforce demands.

## Stakes
Competitors like Workday had functional applications on the iPad.

## Discovery
Not all services needed to be manageable on all devices; some HR tasks were too complex for practical tablet implementation.

## Outcome
Peoplefluent launched a series of iPad apps, keeping its HRM offerings competitive.
