Led design & development of a system to track compliance constraints on hedge fund investments.

## Problem
Hedge funds often had to wait for specific conditions before executing deals. Research for a deal could precede execution by many months, but deals still had to go through compliance and meet other constraints.

## Stakes
Executing deals with invalid compliance constraints could result in significant financial and reputational risks.

## Discovery
To adapt to evolving regulatory environments and complex investment instruments, constraints on a hedge had to vary. This needed to be balanced against simplicity of use for researchers, compliance staff, and management.

## Outcome
Implemented a compliance tracking system, protecting the firm from regulatory breaches and enhancing operational transparency.
