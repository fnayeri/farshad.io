## Change Format

Each entry includes:
- **#** – Sequential change number
- **Date** – When the change was made
- **Type** – `edit` | `add` | `remove` | `column` (structural)
- **Target** – Row identifier (name/permalink) or "all"
- **Description** – What was changed
- **Before** → **After** – For edits; exact values when helpful
